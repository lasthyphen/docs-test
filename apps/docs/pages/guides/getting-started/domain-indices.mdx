Below is the list of what we call "Dijetal Domains" & their Indices. Each domain represents one or more product or service 
value proposition to end-users and/or businesses that Dijets can provide **TODAY** on any of its ternary chain ledgers (except the indices marked
with an asterisk denoting their use for Governance purposes only).

The Domains are also used by Dijets governance council to include in the preamble of the [DIPs](https://dips.dijets.io)

The expectation is that the list will undergo moderation as the ecosystem grows and matures. And with each refinement a changelog of the
amendments made to the domain or its index will be kept up to date.
