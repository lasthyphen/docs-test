Before you begin, please vet your idea, this will save you time . Check first if an idea is original to avoid wasting time on something that’s bound
to be rejected based on prior research (searching the Internet does not always do the trick). Just because an idea sounds good to the author does not mean it will have the intended effect.
The best practice is to keep the big picture in context, without forgetting about the finer details of your idea proposal.
